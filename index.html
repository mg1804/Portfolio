    My Approach

    The LENS Framework for Building Meaningful Products

    L – Listen to the System

    I start by understanding real needs — through data, user interviews, or system analysis. Whether it’s clinician surveys or user journey audits, every product tells a story if you’re willing to listen.

    E – Empathize with Stakeholders

    My solutions begin with empathy. I map goals, frictions, and motivations of everyone involved — from developers to caregivers — to create alignment and build trust in every interaction.

    N – Navigate the Trade-offs

    I bring clarity where priorities collide. By aligning effort with value, I help teams make intentional decisions, balance technical debt with delivery, and move forward with confidence.

    S – Scale What Works

    I close the loop by scaling the proven. Whether through automation, playbooks, or dashboards, I turn insight into repeatable programs that drive results.
